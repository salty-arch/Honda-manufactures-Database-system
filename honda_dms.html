STORED PROCEDURES

4 Procedures

sp_RegisterSale — Atomic Sale Transaction

TRANSACTION + ROLLBACK

Validates vehicle availability, inserts the SalesContract, and marks inventory as Sold — all in one atomic transaction. Rolls back everything if any step fails.

sp_CustomerProfile — Multi-Result Profile

MULTI RESULT SET

Returns three result sets in one call: customer info, all their contracts, and their full service history.

sp_MonthlySalesReport — Revenue Breakdown

AGGREGATION

Generates a detailed monthly sales report for a dealership with a summary row showing total revenue and average sale price.

sp_BookService — Duplicate Guard

VALIDATION LOGIC

Books a service appointment but first checks whether the same VIN already has a Scheduled appointment on that date — prevents duplicates at the database level.

TRIGGERS

4 Triggers

trg_ContractSigned_UpdateInventory

AFTER INSERT/UPDATE

Fires after a SalesContract is inserted or updated. When status = Signed, automatically sets the vehicle's Inventory status to Sold. Demonstrate by checking VIN status before and after signing a contract.

trg_PreventDoubleSale

INSTEAD OF INSERT

Fires instead of a contract INSERT. If the vehicle is already Sold, it raises an error and blocks the insert. Try inserting a contract for a VIN that is already Sold to see the trigger block it.

trg_ContractCancelled_RestoreInventory

AFTER UPDATE

Fires after a contract is updated to Cancelled. Automatically restores the vehicle's Inventory status back to Available. Enter a contract ID to cancel it and watch the VIN revert.

trg_ContractPending_ReserveInventory

AFTER INSERT

Fires after a Pending contract is inserted. Automatically sets the vehicle status to Reserved so it can't be sold to someone else while the contract is being negotiated. Check any VIN's current status below.
